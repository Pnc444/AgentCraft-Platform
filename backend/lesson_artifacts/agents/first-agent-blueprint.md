# The Four-Box Blueprint

**The finish line, defined before you start:** each box holds one honest sentence, and the whole thing reads aloud in under thirty seconds. That is finished. Polished is a different thing, and not today's job.

## Juno's blueprint (the worked example)

- **Goal:** Find three good articles on a topic I give it, and summarize each one.
- **One tool:** Web search (with reading).
- **One memory:** The list of articles found and kept so far.
- **One check + one stop:** Checks each article is really about my topic; stops when three good summaries are delivered, or after ten searches, whichever comes first.

Notice: every box is one plain sentence. That is the target amount of polish.
