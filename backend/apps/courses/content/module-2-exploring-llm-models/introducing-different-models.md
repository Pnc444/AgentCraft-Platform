# Introducing Different Models

## How are LLM Models Different?

LLM models are an application of generative AI. Specifically, they take text as input, and output text based on the data they are trained on. Examples of large language models include Gemini(Google), Claude(Anthropic), and OpenAI's GPT models.

Models may differ in the training data used, as well as other factors.
